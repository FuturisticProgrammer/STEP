About

STEP is a hackathon - an invention competition that usually spans 12 or 24 hours where high school students can collaborate in teams in order to build amazing things in hardware, software, or both.

STEP is the first hackathon to ever be hosted at West Windsor-Plainsboro High School South. The Computer Science Club and the school administration have worked together to create this event from the ground up.

Whether you're a seasoned hackathon goer or just getting started — we’d love to see you here!

Innovate

An idea is the foundation of any hack. Be creative!

Develop

For 12 straight hours, you have the ability to create, tune, and refine your ideas

Share

You have the opportunity to exhibit and share what you've made to the judges and the community

Learn

There will be several workshops hosted by expert mentors taking place during this hackathon, which are amazing ways to pick up new skills and learn new languages.

FAQs

How long is STEP?

13 hours from check-in to the end of closing ceremonies. Hacking is 10 hours long.

What do I need to bring?

A laptop/computer, an ID, and any hardware you plan on using in your hack.

How do teams work?

Teams are composed of 1-4 people. Although forming a team beforehand is encouraged, if you don't have one, there will be an hour dedicated to team formation at the beginning of the hackathon.

What can I make?

Anything you want - hardware, software, or a mix!

What can I win?

Prizes are TBA but we promise they will be amazing.

Is there travel reimbursement?

Unfortunately, we are unable to provide travel reimbursements this year.
